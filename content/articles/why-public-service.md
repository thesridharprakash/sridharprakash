---
title: "Why Public Service Matters"
summary: "Public service is about integrity, responsibility, and putting citizens first."
date: "25-01-2025"
---

Public service is a calling that requires dedication, integrity, and accountability. Leaders who prioritize citizens above self-interest ensure sustainable development and trust in governance.

### Key Principles

- **Integrity:** Honest decision-making.
- **Responsibility:** Delivering promises effectively.
- **Citizen-First:** Always prioritize public interest.
